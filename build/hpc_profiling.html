HPC Architectures

Summary

The basic timing we did last time is a good tool to have, but sometimes it’s better to profile your code a bit more comprehensively. Here, we’ll look at how to use tools like gprof to figure out where execution bottlenecks may be hurting your program’s performance.

Learning Outcomes

  • Students will be able to choose the proper tool.

Essential Questions

  • How fast can I actually go, and which things are stopping me?

Resources

The gprof tool is readily accessible and gives substantial information about run-time performance of programs. Googles gperftools is another great tool.

There are numerous other performance tools available. Valgrind includes cachegrind and callgrind that are useful tools for understanding execution in more detail.

Evidence of Student Learning

  • Students will use gprof and gperftools to profile a C++ program.
  • Students will reflect on their learning by completing their daily log.
